Historical places in Cape Town

City Hall

  • Nelson Mandela made his first speech after he was released from prison on the 11 Feb 1990

  • City hall was built in 1905

  • Built from honey-coloured oolitic limestone imported from England

The Castle of Good Hope

  • Oldest existing colonial building in South Africa

  • It's position marks the original shoreline

  • Build by soldiers and volunteers, slaves and Khoi undergoing punishment

Iziko South African Museum

  • first museum in the country

  • museum houses important African Zoology, paleontology and archeology collections

  • The museum name "Iziko" is a Xhosa word meaning "hearth"
